No blocking of light

We will have one outdoor field in a tent. The way to the outdoor field is open to the air (and rain).

No blocking of light

The technical committee can delimit a zone near the field where humans must not stand and where any items blocking the light sources are forbidden.

No blocking of light

The head referee may decide at any point before or during a game to relocate any objects around the field, or direct persons to another position around the field.

Indirect Kick

Champions Cup

Any team may only score a goal via indirect kick from any restart into Play.
  • Kick-off
  • Kick-in
  • Pushing free kick
    (a.k.a. Foul)
  • Corner kick
  • Goal kick
  • after an unsuccessful Penalty kick

Indirect Kick

Champions Cup

  • No score of a goal from direct kick, including deflections.
  • Ball must be deliberately played a second time.

Standard Penalty Procedure

Standard Penalty Procedure

A robot making reasonable effort to return to the field from a standard penalty must not be penalized for illegal position.

Player stance

  • Up to one robot hand wider on each side
  • Penalty after 10 seconds

Interleaved reffing

  1. Pregame meeting on both fields
  2. First half on field A
  3. All Refs switch field, first half on field B
  4. All Refs switch back, second half on field A
  5. All Refs switch back, second half on field B

Pregame Meeting

  • 10 minutes before the game starts
  • Decide jersey colors
  • Field-Side selection and Initial Kick-Off by coin toss
  • Any other game related topic

New Gamestate: Standby

  • Only in Champions Cup
  • Between Initial and Ready
  • Give Glove signal
  • Callout: Forbidden Motion in Standby color number
  • Should last between 10 and 120 seconds

Gloves

Only in Champions Cup, in Standby:

  • Wear red gloves
  • Stand at the T-junction
  • Raise both hands over your head
  • Hold up for 2 seconds
