Field - Inside and Outside

  • goal lines and touchlines belong to field
  • goal area: surrounding lines are part of the goal area
  • penalty area: surrounding lines are part of the penalty area
  • center circle lines belong to center circle
  • Halfway line does not belong to any half
  • goal line does not belong to goal

Jerseys

  • Up to 5 (CS) or 7 (CC) players
  • Jersey numbers from 1 to 20
  • When playing with full team, one must be a goalkeeper
  • Field players and goalkeeper can be substituted
  • Goalkeepers must have different color than both teams' field players
  • Head ref decides in case of a clash
  • Goalkeeper can wear number 1

Pregame Meeting

  • 10 minutes before the game starts
  • Decide for jersey colors
  • Field-Side selection and Initial Kick-Off by coin toss
  • Any other game related topic

Whistles

  • Entering Play: 1 whistle from the T-junction
    • Begin of a half
    • Following a goal scored
    • Penalty kicks
  • 1st half ends: 2 short whistles from the T-junction
  • 2nd half ends: 2 short 1 long whistles from the T-junction
  • Goal scored: 1 whistle from the field end besides the goal

Indirect Kick

Any attacking team may only score a goal via indirect kick from any restart into Play from a free kick (Does not apply to kick-off and penalty kick).
  • no score of a goal from direct kick, including deflections
  • deliberately played a second time

Goal area positions

  • Not more than 3 defenders are allowed during Set and Play
